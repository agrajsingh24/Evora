# 📖 Overview

**EVORA AI** is an AI-powered Civic Intelligence and ESG Sustainability Platform that enables governments, municipalities, organizations, and citizens to collaboratively monitor, report, manage, and resolve environmental and civic challenges through a unified digital ecosystem.

The platform transforms traditional civic management into an intelligent, data-driven system by combining:

* 🤖 Artificial Intelligence
* 👁 Computer Vision
* 🌍 Environmental Intelligence
* 📊 Real-Time Analytics
* 📡 IoT Integration
* 🗺 GIS & Location Intelligence
* 🎮 Gamification
* 📈 ESG Monitoring

EVORA AI empowers every stakeholder—from citizens reporting issues to administrators making strategic decisions—with intelligent automation, transparency, and actionable insights.

# ❗ Problem Statement

Urban administrations continue to face significant operational and environmental challenges due to fragmented systems and manual workflows.

Key challenges include:

* Manual complaint reporting
* Delayed issue resolution
* Poor waste management
* Limited citizen engagement
* Lack of transparency
* Inefficient workforce coordination
* Inconsistent ESG reporting
* Absence of centralized analytics
* Limited environmental monitoring
* Slow data-driven decision making

These inefficiencies increase operational costs, reduce public trust, and negatively impact environmental sustainability.

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

EVORA AI introduces an integrated AI-powered ecosystem that connects citizens, municipal authorities, environmental agencies, and field workers through intelligent automation.

The platform enables users to:

* Report civic and environmental issues
* Detect and classify issues using AI
* Schedule waste collection
* Monitor complaints in real time
* Track workforce operations
* Analyze environmental trends
* Generate sustainability insights
* Improve governance through data-driven intelligence

By combining Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, GIS, IoT, and advanced analytics, EVORA AI delivers a smarter and more efficient approach to civic management.
